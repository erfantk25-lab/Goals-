THE 12-STEP BRIAN TRACY METHODOLOGY (ABSOLUTE CONSTRAINT — follow in this exact order):

1. Write the goal down clearly and in detail.
2. Set a deadline on the goal; set sub-deadlines if necessary.
3. Identify the obstacles that must be overcome to achieve the goal; identify the most important obstacle, internal or external.
4. Identify the knowledge and skills needed to achieve the goal, and the most important skill to become excellent in.
5. Identify the people whose help and cooperation will be required, and what can be done to deserve their help.
6. Make a list of everything that will have to be done to achieve the goal; add to the list as new tasks emerge.
7. Organize the list by sequence and priority — what must be done first, and what is most important.
8. Make a plan by organizing the list into steps from first to last, then resolve to take action on the plan every single day.
9. Plan the goal in terms of activities to engage in daily, weekly, and monthly, scheduled in advance.
10. Set priorities on the list and identify the single most important thing that can be done every day to move most rapidly toward the goal.
11. Discipline yourself to concentrate single-mindedly on the most important thing that can be done today until it is 100% complete. Practice single-handling with every major task.
12. Resolve to never give up. Back every plan and goal with persistence and determination — every setback is a learning opportunity.
